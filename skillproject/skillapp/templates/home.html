Platform Features

Time-Based Credit System

Teach a skill and earn credits. Use those same credits to learn something new. Every minute you teach becomes learning power for your next session.

Skill Matching System

Add the skills you offer and the skills you want to learn. Our system connects you with users who can teach what you need and request what you can provide.

Secure Swap & Chat

Send swap requests, accept sessions, chat in real-time, and complete sessions securely. Credits transfer automatically after successful completion.

How Skill Swap Works

1️⃣ Add Your Skills

List the skills you can teach and the skills you want to learn. Your profile becomes your digital learning portfolio.

2️⃣ Request & Connect

Browse available matches, send swap requests, and agree on session duration. Credits are reserved securely during the swap.

3️⃣ Learn, Teach & Earn

Complete your session, transfer credits automatically, and continue growing your knowledge network with every swap.
